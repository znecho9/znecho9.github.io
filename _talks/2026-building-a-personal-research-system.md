This talk outline introduces a practical system for independent research:
capturing questions, building small artifacts, writing public notes, and
reviewing progress over time.

## Proposed Structure

1. The problem with scattered notes and unfinished prototypes.
2. How to design a simple research loop.
3. What to publish early, what to keep private, and how to revise.

This draft frames independent engineering as a research practice rather than a
sequence of isolated projects. The central question is how one person can build
software, write notes, and maintain experiments in a way that compounds over
time.

## Outline

1. Why engineering work often fails to become durable knowledge.
2. How notebooks, prototypes, and public writing can form a research loop.
3. What lightweight infrastructure is needed for independent researchers.

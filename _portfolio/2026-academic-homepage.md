This project reorganizes the site around academic profile conventions: an About
homepage, publication-style entries, talks, teaching materials, projects, a CV,
and a chronological writing archive.

## Goals

- Make the site readable as a research profile rather than only a blog.
- Keep the implementation small and maintainable.
- Leave clear placeholders for future publications, talks, and teaching notes.
